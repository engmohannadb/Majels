Problem Statement

The Need for Centralized Management

The platform addresses critical shortcomings in property management, including reliance on disconnected systems and lack of standardized processes.

Fragmented Management

Property owners and shareholders use multiple, disconnected systems (spreadsheets, messaging apps, paper records) to manage real estate assets.

Lack of Transparency

There is difficulty tracking decisions, votes, fees, and complaints among stakeholders, leading to inefficiency and missed updates.

Limited Data Utilization

There is no unified access to open data from the General Authority for Real Estate (GAERE) for informed decisions, and reports are hard to maintain.

03
Core Features

Proposed Solution & Comprehensive Features

Majles is a centralized platform designed to support integration, future upgrades, and national-level adoption with real-time updates.

Features: **Meetings scheduling and minutes tracking** and **Voting and decision recording**. Core capabilities include governance and communication tools.

Features: **Suggestions and complaint management** and **Announcements and notifications**. Addresses inefficient communication and lack of transparency.

Features: **Fee tracking and payments**, **Owner/shareholder satisfaction ratings**, **Document storage**, and **Automated performance and financial reports**. Improves accountability.

Feature: **Open Data access** to connect with **GAERE open data** to enrich insights and ensure compliance. The system also ensures privacy and security.

AI Integration: **Predictive analytics for property performance** and **Automated summarization of meetings and reports**.

AI Integration: **Intelligent assistant for answering property-related queries** and **Analysis on satisfaction and complaints**.

04
Purpose & Tech

Project Strategy & Architecture

Majles is built to be a secure, open-source property management system designed on a unified, high-performance platform for effective governance and operational efficiency.

The system uses MySQL or compatible databases, with API integration. It's designed for future expansion, customization, and integration with national systems.

Open-Source & Cloud-Native

High-performance architecture on a unified platform.

Advanced Stakeholder Coverage

Our system is intended for **property owners, administrators, and shareholders**, covering all stages of property management.

Data Security & Compliance

Security is prioritized with **role-based access**, encrypted data, and compliance with real estate standards. It is designed to ensure compliance with the General Authority for Real Estate (GAERE).

Solutions are segmented into executive summary, key features, workflows, and next steps for implementation and scaling.

05
Adoption Structure

Operational Levels and Tiers

The platform is designed to operate seamlessly across different scales and business needs, ready for local and national adoption.

Operational Levels

The system operates across three levels: **Local unit, local district, and city-level** property management.

Goal-Level Tier

A tier for all users with **minimal functionality concerning usage and registration**. This offers baseline access to platform features.

Enterprise & SME Tiers

Dedicated tiers for different business uses, providing advanced customization and full support for larger organizations and small-to-medium enterprises.
